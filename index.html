Crochet abbreviations

  • YO- Yarn over
  • Sl st- Slip stitch
  • Ch- Chain stitch
  • MR- Magic ring
  • SC- Single crochet
  • DC- Double crochet
  • HDC- Half double crochet
  • Tr- Treble crochet
  • DEC- Decrease
  • INC- Increase
  • FLO- Front loop only
  • BLO- Back loop only
  • Rep- Repeat
  • Sk- sKip
  • WS- wrong side
  • RS- Right side
  • BO- Bobble stitch
  • F.O- Fasten off


No.1 Crochet sour gummy

  • make a slip knot
  • ch 2
  • sc 4 in first chain
  • inc in each stitch (8sts)
  • row 1-3: SC around
  • insert eyes around row 2
  • row 4-10: sc around
  • change to second colour
  • row 11-20: SC around
  • bend wire or pipe cleaners to the length of the worm and insert
  • row 21 : decx4
  • cut yarn and secure last stitch
  • use a tapestry needle to sew the small hole shut
  • make a secure knot and weave in the yarn ends

    • No.2 Crochet little frog

    • R1: 6Sc in MR (6)
    • R2: INC x6 (12)
    • R3: Sc, inc, BO, INC, (SC, INC)x2, BO, INC, SC, INC (18)
    • R4: (2SC, INC) Rep x6 (24)
    • R5-7: SC around (24)
    • Add eyes
    • R8: (2SC,DEC) Rep x6 (18)
    • R9: (SC,DEC) Rep x6 (12)
    • Stuff
    • R10: DEC x6 and FO
    • Add a mouth

    No.3 Crochet puffy flower

    • 12 SC into MR
    • Sl st into first SC to join
    • Cut and join next colour
    • Ch 3
    • In the first stitch, YO and pull up a loop
    • YO and pull through two loops
    • Rep 2 more times
    • Next stitch, Rep 3 times
    • YO and pull through all loops
    • Ch3 , Sl st into thesame stitch
    • Rep 5 more times
    • Cut and fasten off

    No. 4 Crochet moon

    • MR
    • R1: Ch2, 14HDC, Sl st
    • R2: Ch2, 2HDC in each stitch
    • R3: Ch2, 2HDC in each stitch
    • Fold the circle, sew and fill in with cotton

    Crochet Jellyfish

    Head part 1

    • R1:6 SC into MR
    • R2: INC x6
    • R3: (1 SC, INC) x6
    • R4: (INC, 2SC) x6
    • R5: (3SC, INC) x6
    • R6: ( INC, 4SC) x6
    • R7-12: 36 SC around
    • R13: (4SC, DEC) x6
    • R14: FLO (3HDC in the next loop, 1 Sl st) x15

    Head part 2

    • R1: 6SC into MR
    • R2: INC x6
    • R3: (1SC, INC) x6
    • R4: (INC, 2SC) x6
    • R5: (3SC, INC) x6
    • leave a long tail for sewing. Cut the yarn and fasten off

    Tentacles x3 Long

    • CH 40
    • 1SC in 2nd ch from the hook, 1SC in every next ch to the end
    • FO and cut the yarn

    Tentacles x6 Short

    • CH 32
    • Rep steps in Long tentacles

    Assembly

    • Sew the thntacles to part 2: sew the long ones directly onto every second SC of the 1st round, and the short ones- of the 2nd round so all of them spread out. Fasten off on the wrong side of Part 2 with knots, cut off the yarn.
    • If you are using plastic eyes with a mount, insert and secure them before the next step.
    • Sew together part 1 (under the remaining back loops of the 13th round) and part 2 (under the back loops) parts of the head by 2/3, stuff the head, then sew the remaining third.
    • Sew the eyes (at a distance of about 5-6 inches between them, in the lower third of the head)
