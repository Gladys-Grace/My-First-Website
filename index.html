Crochet abbreviations

  • YO- Yarn over
  • Sl st- Slip stitch
  • Ch- Chain stitch
  • SC- Single crochet
  • DC- Double crochet
  • HDC- Half double crochet
  • Tr- Treble crochet
  • Dec- Decrease
  • Inc- Increase
  • FLO- Front loop only
  • BLO- Back loop only
  • Rep- Repeat
  • Sk- sKip
  • Ws- wrong side
  • RS- Right side
  • F.O- Fasten off


No.1 Crochet sour gummy

  • make a slip knot
  • ch 2
  • sc 4 in first chain
  • inc in each stitch (8sts)
  • row 1-3: SC around
  • insert eyes around row 2
  • row 4-10: sc around
  • change to second colour
  • row 11-20: SC around
  • bend wire or pipe cleaners to the length of the worm and insert
  • row 21 : decx4
  • cut yarn and secure last stitch
  • use a tapestry needle to sew the small hole shut
  • make a secure knot and weave in the yarn ends
